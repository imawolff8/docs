NextNav is a lightweight, self-hosted bookmark manager that gives you a single place to store, organize, and search all your links — accessible from any device, browser, or operating system. Whether you're switching between a work laptop, a home desktop, or a phone, your bookmarks follow you without depending on a cloud service, browser sync, or a third-party account.

## Key Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Cross-Device Access" icon="mobile-screen">
    Open your bookmarks from any device — desktop, tablet, or phone — through a standard web browser. No app installs or browser extensions required.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Cross-Browser Compatible" icon="browsers">
    Because NextNav runs as a web app, it works identically in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and any other modern browser.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Self-Hosted and Private" icon="lock">
    Your data never leaves your own server. NextNav stores everything locally in SQLite, so you stay in full control of your bookmarks and browsing history.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Categories and Organization" icon="folder-open">
    Group bookmarks into custom categories and subcategories to keep large collections tidy and easy to navigate.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Fast Search" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Instantly find any saved link by title, URL, or keyword. The built-in search scans your entire library without leaving the page.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Import Existing Bookmarks" icon="file-import">
    Bring your existing bookmarks in with a single import step. NextNav supports standard browser export formats so you don't start from scratch.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy NextNav on your server">
    Spin up NextNav using a single Docker command or by dropping the PHP files onto an existing web server. The whole process takes under five minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your admin account">
    The first time you visit your NextNav URL, a setup wizard walks you through creating an admin username and password. No external registration needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add and access your bookmarks">
    Save links from the admin panel, import a browser export file, or install the browser extension to clip pages on the fly. Your bookmarks are instantly available from any device.
  </Step>
</Steps>
