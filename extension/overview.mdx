The NextNav browser extension connects your browser directly to your self-hosted NextNav instance, so you can save bookmarks without ever leaving the page you're on. Whether you're researching, reading, or browsing, adding a new bookmark takes a single click from your toolbar.

## What the Extension Does

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="One-Click Save" icon="bookmark">
    Click the NextNav extension icon on any page to instantly save the current URL to your NextNav instance — no copying, pasting, or tab-switching required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Auto-Fill" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    The extension automatically pre-fills the bookmark name, URL, and favicon from the current page, so you spend less time typing and more time browsing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Category Picker" icon="folder-open">
    Choose exactly which NextNav category to file your bookmark in right from the extension popup, keeping your library organized from the moment you save.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Instant Access" icon="arrow-up-right-from-square">
    Open your full NextNav dashboard directly from the extension popup whenever you need to browse, search, or manage your entire bookmark collection.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Supported Browsers

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Chrome">
    The NextNav extension is available on the **Chrome Web Store**. Search for "NextNav" or follow the direct link from your admin panel under **Settings** → **Browser Extension** to find and install it in one click.

    <Note>
      Chrome Web Store availability depends on your NextNav release. If the extension has not yet been published for your version, follow the [manual unpacked-extension install](/extension/setup) instructions instead.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Firefox">
    Install the NextNav extension from **Firefox Add-ons** (addons.mozilla.org). Search for "NextNav" and add it to Firefox — the same core extension powers all supported browsers.

    <Note>
      Firefox Add-ons availability depends on your NextNav release. If the listing is not yet live for your version, follow the [manual unpacked-extension install](/extension/setup) instructions instead.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Edge">
    Find the NextNav extension in the **Microsoft Edge Add-ons** store. Search for "NextNav" and click **Get** to install it. Edge also supports Chrome Web Store extensions if you prefer that route.

    <Note>
      Microsoft Edge Add-ons availability depends on your NextNav release. If the extension has not yet been published for your version, follow the [manual unpacked-extension install](/extension/setup) instructions instead.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  The extension requires your NextNav instance to be running and accessible from your browser. Make sure you've completed the [deployment steps](/deployment/docker) before installing the extension.
</Note>
