## What NextNav does

NextNav solves the problem of bookmarks being scattered across multiple browsers and devices. Instead of syncing bookmarks through each browser's built-in account, you keep everything in one place that you control.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy NextNav">
    Run NextNav on your server using Docker or a manual PHP/SQLite setup. See [Deploy with Docker](/deployment/docker) or [Manual Installation](/deployment/manual).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your account">
    On first launch, NextNav walks you through creating your admin username and password. Your credentials are stored locally — no external accounts needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your bookmarks">
    Import your existing bookmarks from Chrome, Firefox, or Edge, or start adding them one by one. Install the browser extension to save new bookmarks with a single click.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  Looking for the fastest path? Follow the [Quick Start guide](/quickstart) to go from zero to a running NextNav instance in under five minutes.
